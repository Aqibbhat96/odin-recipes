Strawberry Bellini

Description

A nice drink for Mother's Day, a brunch, or a shower.

A Bellini is a cocktail made with Prosecco and peach pure or nectar. It originated in Venice, Italy.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • ¼ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brandy
  • 1 ½ cups chilled sparkling wine
  • 3 large strawberries

Steps

  1. Step 1 - Blend 3 cups strawberries, confectioners' sugar, and brandy together in a blender until smooth. Chill in refrigerator for 10 minutes.
  2. Step 2 - Divide the strawberry mixture among 3 champagne flutes; top each with about 1/2 cup sparkling wine; stir. Garnish each glass with 1 strawberry.
