Frozen Derby Mint Juleps

Description

Perfect for Derby Day or any day spent lounging in the sun, these cool refreshing drinks are dangerously delicious! Garnish with fresh mint.

It's a refreshing combination of mint, sugar, bourbon and water, perfect for sipping on hot days. The major difference between it and the Mojito is that it uses bourbon instead of rum.

Ingredients

  • 6 fluid ounces bourbon
  • 2 ½ fluid ounces lemon juice
  • 2 fluid ounces simple syrup
  • 20 mint leaves
  • 4 cups ice

Steps

  1. Step 1 - Pour bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and mint leaves into a blender; blend on high until combined. Add ice; blend until juleps have a slushy consistency. Divide among serving glasses.
  2. Step 2 - Enjoy the drink
